## 1. How to Choose a Perfume

Finding your signature scent is a personal journey. Here's how to start:

- **Know your scent family** — Floral, woody, oriental, fresh. Which one calls to you?
- **Test on your skin** — Fragrances react differently with your body chemistry. Always try before you buy.
- **Give it time** — Let the fragrance settle. Top notes fade after 15–30 minutes; the heart and base notes reveal the true character.
- **Consider the mood** — What feeling do you want to carry with you? Calming lavender for relaxation? Warm vanilla for comfort?

## 2. How to Use a Diffuser

Get the most out of your Miss Vee home fragrance:

- **Placement matters** — Position your diffuser in a central spot where air circulates (not directly in sunlight or near a radiator).
- **Flip the reeds** — Turn the reeds over once a week to refresh the scent.
- **Size your space** — A standard 100ml reed diffuser works beautifully in bedrooms, bathrooms, and home offices.
- **Safety first** — Keep out of reach of children and pets.

## 3. Scent Families Explained

- **Floral** — Rose, jasmine, lavender, chamomile. Romantic, calming, timeless.
- **Woody** — Sandalwood, cedar, patchouli. Grounding, warm, sophisticated.
- **Oriental** — Vanilla, amber, musk. Sensual, rich, comforting.
- **Fresh** — Citrus, green notes, aquatic. Energising, clean, uplifting.

## 4. Creating Your Self-Care Ritual

Transform your daily routine into a moments of calm:

- **Morning** — Start with a fresh, citrus spritz to energise your space
- **Midday** — Take three deep breaths with a calming floral scent
- **Evening** — Set the mood with a warm, woody diffuser as you wind down
- **Before bed** — A spritz of lavender on your pillow for restful sleep
