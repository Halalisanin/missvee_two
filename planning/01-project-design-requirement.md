## 2. Why Sanctuary Spa?

Sanctuary Spa is a well-established UK brand that started in Covent Garden as a treatment space for women to relax and pamper themselves. Their website is described as a "sensory site design" that combines beautiful imagery with powerful eCommerce features.

### How This Translates to Miss Vee Perfume

Sanctuary's approach is ideal for Miss Vee because:
- Both brands are **lady-owned / female-focused**
- Both sell **luxurious, sensory products** (fragrances for body and home)
- Both benefit from a **wellness / self-care positioning**
- The **botanical, calming aesthetic** works beautifully for perfume and diffuser products
- The **values-driven messaging** (natural, ethical) builds trust for a new business

## 5. Homepage Layout

**Section 1: Hero Banner** — Full-width calming lifestyle image. Tagline: *"Self-Care is You. In Your Home. In Your Sanctuary."* CTA: "Discover Your Sanctuary"

**Section 2: Brand Values Strip** — Horizontal strip: Natural Ingredients, Mindful Self-Care, Woman-Owned, Sustainable Packaging

**Section 3: Category Tiles** — "For Your Skin" (perfumes) and "For Your Home" (diffusers) with soft, botanical imagery

**Section 4: Featured Products** — "Create Your Self-Care Ritual" — grid of 4–6 products

**Section 5: Brand Story Snapshot** — Short paragraph about Miss Vee + calming image

**Section 6: Trust Signals** — Quality Ingredients, Carefully Curated, Satisfaction Guaranteed, Secure Shopping

**Section 7: Testimonials** — 2–3 customer reviews

**Section 8: Newsletter** — "Join the Sanctuary" with 10% off offer

## 6. Product Page Layout

**Left Column:** Main product image (soft, natural lighting) + thumbnail gallery

**Right Column:** Product name, price, "The Scent Experience" sensory description, scent notes graphic, size selector, quantity, "Add to Cart" button (Sanctuary Orange), secure checkout badge

**Below the Fold:** "The Ritual" storytelling description, key features, shipping info, cross-sell section "Complete Your Sanctuary"

## 9. Key Design Features (Sanctuary-Inspired)

1. **Sensory Design Approach** — Calming, sensory feel through soft colours and beautiful imagery
2. **Wellness Positioning** — Products as self-care rituals, not just beauty products
3. **Values Strip** — Prominent brand values display (natural, ethical, woman-owned)
4. **Calming Colour Palette** — Soft creams, sage greens, Sanctuary Orange accent
5. **Botanical Elements** — Soft, organic design with watercolour and floral motifs
6. **Storytelling Focus** — Strong brand story and product narratives
7. **Cross-Sell Integration** — "Complete Your Sanctuary" matching section
8. **Dummy Checkout Flow** — Fully visual prototype for client demonstrations
