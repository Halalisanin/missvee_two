About Miss Vee

This is the story behind the scents.

Our Story

I have always loved fragrance. Not in a decorative "collecting pretty bottles" way, but in a real way — how a good scent can shift your mood, spark a memory, or make you stand a little taller. That feeling is what Miss Vee is built on.

I am based in Ekurhuleni, Gauteng, and everything I make comes from a simple belief: you do not need a special occasion to smell good. You just need a scent that feels like you.

We are new. But we are here to stay. I would love for you to be part of this journey.
