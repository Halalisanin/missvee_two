# Miss Vee Perfume — Concept Two: "Botanical Wellness & Self-Care"

**Client:** Miss Vee Perfume (Lady-owned start-up)  
**Location:** Ekurhuleni, Gauteng, South Africa  
**Business Type:** Fragrance Retail — Personal Perfumes + Home Diffusers  
**Design Inspiration:** Sanctuary Spa (uk.sanctuary.com)  
**Project Status:** Planning & Discovery Phase

---

## Overview

Concept Two positions Miss Vee Perfume as a **wellness and self-care brand**, inspired by Sanctuary Spa's calming, botanical, and sensory design approach. The website mirrors the spa experience — soft colours, botanical imagery, and a focus on emotional wellbeing through scent.

## Product Categories

- **Perfumes (Personal Fragrance)** — Eau de Parfum and Eau de Toilette
- **Diffusers (Home Fragrance)** — Reed diffusers and room sprays
- **Gift Sets** — Curated bundles for self-care gifting

## Brand Positioning

> *"Self-Care is You. In Your Home. In Your Sanctuary."*

Miss Vee fragrances are positioned as daily wellness rituals — not just beauty products. The brand emphasises natural ingredients, mindful living, and the calming power of scent.
