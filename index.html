Miss Vee Perfume

Concept Two — Botanical Wellness & Self-Care

“Self-Care is You. In Your Home. In Your Sanctuary.”

Project

Fragrance eCommerce website for Miss Vee Perfume — a lady-owned start-up based in Ekurhuleni, Gauteng, South Africa. Selling personal perfumes and home diffusers.

Design Inspiration

Sanctuary Spa (uk.sanctuary.com). Calming, botanical, wellness-focused sensory design with a warm, nurturing aesthetic.
