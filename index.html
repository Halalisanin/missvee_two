About Miss Vee

Miss Vee Perfume is a fragrance eCommerce brand selling personal perfumes and home diffusers. A space for botanical wellness and sensory self-care, rooted in the belief that fragrance is more than scent—it is ritual, memory, and sanctuary.

A lady-owned start-up based in Ekurhuleni, Gauteng, South Africa.
